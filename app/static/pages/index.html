Frequently Asked Questions

How does MyResumeMatch create ATS-friendly resumes?

MyResumeMatch uses advanced AI to analyze job descriptions and match your experience with required keywords. Our system ensures your resume passes through Applicant Tracking Systems by optimizing formatting, keywords, and structure that recruiters and AI systems look for.

Can MyResumeMatch write personalized cover letters?

Yes! Our AI crafts unique cover letters for each job application by analyzing both your resume and the job description. The result is a compelling narrative that connects your specific achievements to the employer's needs, significantly increasing interview rates.

What resume templates does MyResumeMatch offer?

We provide professional, ATS-tested templates including Harvard, Tech, Modern, and Executive styles. Each template is optimized for readability and keyword density, ensuring your resume looks professional while passing automated screening systems.

How quickly can I generate a resume and cover letter?

With MyResumeMatch, you can generate both a tailored resume and cover letter in under 60 seconds. Simply upload your existing resume, paste the job description, and download your professionally optimized documents instantly.

Is MyResumeMatch free to use?

MyResumeMatch offers 5 free credits to start, allowing you to generate up to 5 complete resume and cover letter packages. After that, we have affordable pricing plans for continued access to our AI-powered career tools.
