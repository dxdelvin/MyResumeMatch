Frequently Asked Questions

How do I generate a resume from a job description?

Simply paste your target job description into ResumeAI. Our AI analyzes the keywords and requirements, then rewrites your existing experience to match the role perfectly, increasing your ATS score.

Can AI write a cover letter for a specific job?

Yes. ResumeAI reads the job description and your resume simultaneously to craft a persuasive cover letter that explains exactly why your specific skills make you the right fit for that specific job.

How can I improve my old CV with AI?

Upload your old CV PDF. Our AI will extract the text, modernize the formatting to Harvard or Tech styles, and suggest improvements to bullet points to make them result-oriented and impactful.
